Instructions for Survey

 

In this HIT, you will be shown 11 pairs of screenshots and accompanying descriptions of these screenshots. These descriptions may either describe the functionality implemented by an entire screen (e.g., "This app displays a login page for a weather app"), or by specific components on the screen (e.g., "There is a gear icon in the top left corner of screen to access settings").

Your goal is to provide feedback about how well the description describes the functionality of the paired screenshots. You will provide feedback for each screenshot-description pair in two ways. First you will answer a series of three multiple choice requestions that will allow you to rate the accuracy, information, and understandability of the description in describing the functionality of the screenshot. Second, you will answer a set of three free form questions to provide some information regarding the accuracies and inaccuracies of the description as well as suggestions for improvements.

*Note that you must provide high quality responses to the free form text questions in order for the HIT to be accepted.
